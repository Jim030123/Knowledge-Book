# What are the Schematic editor
- To draw the schematic of the PCB. 
- KiCad is flexible enough and allows you to create PCBs without a schematic.
**Captures all neccessary information that the layout editor uses:**
- Components (Symbols)
- Wires that connect pins nets 
- Netlabels
- Busses
- Power nets
